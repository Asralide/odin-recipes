Cafe Au Lait

I'm somewhat new to coffee, so I'm learning to make it in all sorts of different ways. How many ways are there to make coffee, all-in-all? I have no clue! Probably too many.

Cafe Au Lait, simple enough, coffee and steamed milk. Perfect for a cold winters day.

Ingredients

  • 1 part hot strong coffee, French Roast
  • 1 part milk, steamed

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Into a large cup, pour equal parts of strong hot coffee and steamed milk, reserving the foam.
  3. Stir.
  4. Spoon reserved foam on top and serve hot.
